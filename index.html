Understanding Prefix and Postfix Evaluation

Prefix notation: requires that all operators precede the operands that they work on.

Postfix notation: requires that all operators follow the operands that they work on.

Prefix Evaluation

Postfix Evaluation

Expression: 9 2 3 * +

  2. Add 9 + 6 = 15.
